About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, India. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India
  • Built: 1632-1653
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • One of the New Seven Wonders of the World

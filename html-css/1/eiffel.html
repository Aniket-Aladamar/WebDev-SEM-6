The Eiffel Tower

Quick Facts

  • Location: Paris, France
  • Built: 1887-1889
  • Height: 324 meters (1,063 ft)
  • Designer: Gustave Eiffel

About

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris. It is one of the world's most recognizable landmarks and has become a global symbol of France.

Visitor Information

The tower is open to visitors daily. You can climb to different levels for spectacular views of Paris.
